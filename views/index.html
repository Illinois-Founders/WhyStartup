About

Sazme is a web platform that will allow users around the world to communicate with one and another anonymous.
Ever wanted to talk to someone about something, but have no one to share your problems with?
Have you ever wanted to write your heart out without the fear of being judged?
Join Sazme now and vent your heart out.

Features

Completely Anonymous

At sazme, keeping your identity anonymous is our first priority.

Limitless Categories

We know that each and every one of us have our own problems and experiences. At sazme, we encourage you to create new categories.

Community That Cares

By connecting people who have undergone the same experiences, we are trying to foster a community with people who support one another.

Accessible To All

We want sazme to be easily accessed by everyone, we are currently trying to create accessibity mode which will allow text to be enlarged or read out!

Write Your Heart Out

Regardless of whether or not you choose to share your stories or to write them in your personalized collection of notes, writting is a form of cathartic release!

Code Protected

To ensure that your profile is highly secure, we are currently implementing an additional layer of security when your screen is left to idle.
